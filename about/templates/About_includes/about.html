About Us

Welcome To Our Website!

Aakash Power Engineering, established in 2004, is a leading provider of energy solutions specializing in energy auditing, power quality, and advanced energysaving technologies. With over 35 years of experience, we offer a wide range of products, including Active Power Filters, Static Var Generators, Solar Hybrid Inverters, and Energy Storage Systems. Aakash Power provides design, engineering, consultancy, installation, and maintenance services for energy efficiency and sustainability. We deliver high-quality, cost-effective solutions while promoting environmental protection and supporting safety certifications. Our goal is to help businesses reduce energy consumption and contribute to a greener future.
